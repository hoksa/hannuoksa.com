Hannu Oksa

I’m a designer living in Brooklyn, New York via Helsinki, Finland.

Currently I’m a Creative Director at Reaktor where I help our clients in digital product design and service development.

Recently I’ve been working with HBO on their streaming products and Michael Kors in concept development in the mobile space.

I’m very critical of software. It should be means to an end only when it’s the most viable solution to a problem. Software should never be built for software’s sake.
